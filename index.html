How we met

We first crossed paths at a local coffee shop in the heart of LA. It was a regular Tuesday morning, but as fate would have it, we reached for the same pastry at the counter, our hands brushing slightly. Pain au chocolat.

Awkward apologies turned into friendly conversation, and soon, we found ourselves sitting at the same table, sharing stories, and laughing as if we had known each other forever. That chance meeting over coffee turned into countless more dates, hikes, and late-night conversations.

Our love story has been built on those everyday moments, from our favorite coffee shop to our shared love for weekend adventures. We've grown together, laughed together, and supported each other through the ups and downs of life. Now, we're excited to take the next step on this incredible journey together and invite you to be a part of our special day.

Events

19th January Morning

Pre-Wedding Family and Close Friends Ceremony 9AM - 11AM

The pre-wedding ceremony will be held strictly between the bride and groom. To honor Marybeth’s Indonesian background they will follow the traditional wedding rituals of Minyak Kayu Putih. This tradition tasks the bride and groom to catch a rooster together with their hands bound behind their backs. It symbolizes the trials they must go through relying on solely each other only within their marriage.

Cocktail Lunch 11AM - 1PM

This intimate gathering will take place at a charming garden venue bathed in natural light. The menu will feature a delightful selection of hors d'oeuvres, from gourmet mini sandwiches to elegant canapés. Live acoustic music will serenade the couple and their loved ones as they toast to their future together.

19th January Evening

Indonesian Payam (for Family only) 3pm-4PM

The Payam ceremony will he a private family event. This tradition will involve the application of turmeric paste on the hands, legs and face of the bride, while the groom must pluck the longest hair he can find on his head. This signifies longevity and a good fortune. The groom is not allowed to see the bride after this ritual until the final wedding ceremony starts.

Wedding 5:30PM-8:30PM

The enchanting venue will be adorned with breathtaking floral arrangements and a picturesque view of Seoul, South Korea under which the couple will exchange their vows. The ceremony will be a beautiful blend of tradition and personal touches, reflecting the unique love story of the bride and groom. With family and friends as witnesses, this moment will be forever etched in their hearts as they embark on their lifelong journey together.
